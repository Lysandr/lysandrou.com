4kN Ethanol/LOX Rocket Propulsion System

Personal Project• Ithaca, NY •August '17

Progress report: I fully switched to a 4kN engine design from a 20kN engine. I decided it might be more technically interesting to have the ability to build a GNC testbed (like Masten!) than an engine for suborbital flight. So far all the equations have been solved and I have a proper mechanical description. I am now working on the thermodynamics calcs and we'll see how they change the design. I'm shooting for a film cooled design as I would rather not have to deal with expensive and complicated machining that usually comes with regenerative systems. I will post updates as they come along.

Drone Flight Computer ~ For ECE3140

Class project• Ithaca, NY • May '17

The goal was to design, build, and test a project using the class-selected embedded platform. My partned and I decided to take it to the next level. We wrote, integrated, and tested a drone flight computer. We programmed the MK64FN1M0 FRDM board to use timer interrupts to read incoming PWM from the 2.4GHz receiver, scale those values, then apply them as new setpoints to the PID rate controller. The PID controller used a I2C gyroscope for direct feedback (no time for fancy estimation methods unfortunately..) to stabilize the system. We implemented a brute force Nichols Ziegler tune. The output of the independant RPY rate controllers were summed up to create a PWM value that was distributed to the brushless electronic speed controllers.
